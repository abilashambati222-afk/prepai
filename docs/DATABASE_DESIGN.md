# PrepAI – Database Design & Schema Models

PrepAI leverages MongoDB Atlas as a persistent object-relational document store, utilizing Mongoose ODM to enforce validations, cast fields, and define relationship schemas between collection tables.

---

## 🗺️ Entity-Relationship Topology

```
   ┌──────────────┐
   │     User     │
   └──────┬───────┘
          │ 1
          ├───────────────────────────┬───────────────────────────┐
          │ 1..*                      │ 0..1                      │ 1..*
   ┌──────▼───────┐            ┌──────▼───────┐            ┌──────▼─────────┐
   │  Interview   │            │    Career    │            │ JobDescription │
   └──────────────┘            └──────────────┘            └────────────────┘
                                      │ 1
                                      │ 1..*
                               ┌──────▼───────┐
                               │CareerSnapshot│
                               └──────────────┘
```

## 🗄️ Database Schemas & Collection Definitions

### 1. Users Collection (`users`)
Represents registered candidates, credentials, and their personalized onboarding profiles.

*   `_id` — `ObjectId` (Primary Key).
*   `fullName` — `String` (Required, max length 100 characters).
*   `email` — `String` (Unique, lowercase, email regex validated).
*   `password` — `String` (Hashed with bcryptjs, excluded from standard queries).
*   `role` — `String` (Default: `'student'`).
*   `profileCompleted` — `Boolean` (Default: `false`).
*   `lastLogin` — `Date`.
*   `phone` — `String` (Optional).
*   **Academic Fields**:
    *   `college` — `String` (Onboarding university name).
    *   `degree` — `String` (e.g., `'B.Tech'`).
    *   `branch` — `String` (e.g., `'Computer Science'`).
    *   `graduationYear` — `Number`.
    *   `cgpa` — `Number`.
*   **Career Customizations**:
    *   `targetRole` — `String` (Dream role, e.g., `'Software Engineer'`).
    *   `targetCompany` — `String` (Dream target company, e.g., `'Google'`).
    *   `preferredDifficulty` — `String` (`'Easy' | 'Medium' | 'Hard'`).
    *   `dailyGoal` — `Number` (Target questions per day).
    *   `programmingLanguages` — `[String]`.
    *   `frameworks` — `[String]`.
    *   `databases` — `[String]`.
    *   `tools` — `[String]`.
    *   `github` / `linkedin` / `portfolio` — `String` URLs.

### 2. Interviews Collection (`interviews`)
Tracks candidate mock interview sessions, dynamic questions generated by Gemini, and real-time scores.

*   `_id` — `ObjectId`.
*   `user` — `ObjectId` (Foreign Key referencing `User`, Indexed).
*   `interviewType` — `String` (`'HR Interview' | 'Technical Interview' | 'Behavioral Interview' | 'Coding Interview' | 'Resume Based Interview' | 'Project Based Interview'`).
*   `company` — `String` (Target company context).
*   `role` — `String` (Target role context).
*   `difficulty` — `String` (`'Easy' | 'Medium' | 'Hard'`).
*   `status` — `String` (`'started' | 'in_progress' | 'completed' | 'ended'`).
*   `duration` — `Number` (Total session time spent in seconds).
*   `questions` — `Array` of `interviewQuestionSchema`:
    *   `questionText` — `String` (Generated AI prompt).
    *   `type` — `String` (`'technical' | 'hr' | 'behavioral' | 'coding' | 'resume' | 'project'`).
    *   `category` — `String` (e.g., `'Database'`, `'OOP'`).
    *   `answer` — `Object` containing `answerText` (String), `submittedAt` (Date), and `duration` (Number).
    *   `feedback` — `Object`:
        *   `score` — `Number` (0-100).
        *   `technicalAccuracy` / `completeness` / `communication` / `confidence` — `Number` (0-100).
        *   `starEvaluation` — STAR structure analysis (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for behavioral sessions.
        *   `explanation` — `String` (AI feedback detail).
        *   `improvementSuggestions` — `[String]`.
*   `analytics` — `Object` containing summary scores and weak/strong topics.
*   `certificate` — `Object` containing `certificateId` (String), and `issuedAt` (Date).

---

### 3. Careers Collection (`careers`)
Tracks the overall career readiness parameters, matching percentages, Gemini-generated study roadmaps, and resources.

*   `_id` — `ObjectId`.
*   `user` — `ObjectId` (Foreign Key referencing `User`, Indexed).
*   `careerScore` — `Number` (Global readiness score).
*   `targetCompanyReadiness` — `Number` (Matching percent for the dream company).
*   `companyReadiness` — `Array` of `companyReadinessSchema`:
    *   `companyName` — `String`.
    *   `readinessPercent` — `Number`.
    *   `readinessLevel` — `String` (`'Low' | 'Medium' | 'High' | 'Ready'`).
    *   `strengths` / `missing` — `[String]`.
    *   `estimatedPrepTime` — `String`.
*   `recommendedRoles` — `Array` of recommended positions and match percentages.
*   `roadmap` — `Object` containing `weeklyGoals` and `monthlyGoals` structured learning plans.
*   `resources` — `Array` of learning materials (courses, books, links).
*   `projects` — `Array` of project recommendations (tech stack, outcomes).
*   `certifications` — `Array` of professional certification suggestions.
*   `salaryPrediction` — Predicted salary range.

---

### 4. CareerSnapshots Collection (`careersnapshots`)
Chronological snapshots tracking user progression curves for plotting.

*   `_id` — `ObjectId`.
*   `user` — `ObjectId` (Foreign Key referencing `User`, Indexed).
*   `careerScore` — `Number` (Score at snapshot time).
*   `skillsCount` — `Number`.
*   `readyCompaniesCount` — `Number`.
*   `skills` — `[String]`.
*   `analyzedAt` — `Date`.

---

### 5. Job Descriptions Collection (`jobdescriptions`)
Custom user-added job descriptions to align skills against.

*   `_id` — `ObjectId`.
*   `user` — `ObjectId` (Foreign Key referencing `User`, Indexed).
*   `title` — `String` (Job title).
*   `company` — `String`.
*   `jobRole` — `String`.
*   `jobDescriptionText` — `String` (Raw job details).
*   `aiAnalysisStatus` — `String` (`'Idle' | 'Analyzing' | 'Analyzed' | 'Failed'`).
*   `aiAnalysisData` — Cached comparison analysis details.
